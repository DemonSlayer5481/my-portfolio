MY TOP ANIME MOVIES OF ALL TIME

The Golden three

1. Koe no katachi

a movie that teaches you to live again. it's about a bully who wants to make amends with his past and self he thinks of dying everyday.
and a girl who got bullied who thinks it's better if she was dead because she's a burden (she's mute) watch what happens when these two get together.

2. I Want To Eat your Pancrease

this movie will teach you how to live. as an introvert you neglect the basic idea of being a human.
you'll find out that what truly matters are others, self goes away, ego? bye-bye. you realize there are many beautiful people to be thankful for.
and a loving god.

3. Rascals don't dream of dreaming girl senpai

despite the title the anime is just wholsome, heartwarming and heart breaking. the movie is another chapter of the anime.
this movie gave me the greatest gift. it tought me the meaning of true kindness and the hidden connection between
pain, tragedy and kindness, compassion. love.
